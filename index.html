Yes, domain and hosting costs are separate. However, we can help you buy and set them up for free as part of the service.

A basic website takes 3-5 days. A complex business website or portfolio takes about 7-10 days depending on requirements.

Yes! All plans come with free support for 1 month to fix any small issues or bugs.
